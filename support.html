Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • How do I convert Unix timestamps to dates?
    Use the main interface to input your Unix timestamp and the app will automatically convert it to a readable date format.
  • How do I switch between local time and UTC?
    Go to the settings and select your preferred timezone.
  • Can I copy and paste timestamps?
    Yes, you can use the copy and paste functionality to quickly manage timestamps.

Troubleshooting

  • The app is not converting timestamps correctly.
    Ensure that you have entered a valid Unix timestamp. Check your settings to confirm you are in the correct timezone.
  • I encountered a bug.
    Please contact us with detailed information about the issue. Include screenshots if possible.
  • The app crashes on startup.
    Try reinstalling the app. If the problem persists, contact support with details about your device and OS version.
